# Divine AI Film Console Design System

## Primary mood
Ultra-premium, black-metallic, sacred-futuristic, cinematic, AI-console, production-house authority.

## Color palette
Black Metallic #030305, Obsidian Glass #07070B, Titanium Charcoal #111118, Midnight Blue Black #080A16, Deep Studio Navy #050817.
Real Molten Gold #D4AF37, Antique Gold #B88A2A, Champagne Gold #E8C766, Liquid Platinum #D8DCE5, Smoked Silver #8E949E.
Divine Cyan #00E7FF, AI Violet #8A4DFF, Sacred Magenta #FF2FD6, Emerald Frequency #00FFB2, Electric Blue #1B6CFF, Solar Amber #FFB84D.

## Typography
Use Cinzel or similar premium serif for brand and cinematic headings. Use Inter or similar clean sans-serif for UI text.

## Layout
Dense premium dashboard layout, not empty minimalism. Keep clean hierarchy: hero console, identity strip, featured releases, album universe, videos/shorts, services, AI console, sonic lab, gallery, news, collaboration/licensing/contact.

## Motion
Loader: black-gold film reel dissolve. Navigation: magnetic underline. Cards: tilt + gold shine. Waveform: gentle pulse. Background: slow divine energy drift. Always include reduced-motion fallback.
